Pan-seared sirloin tips with balsamic glaze
Sofia Pitt
@bottomless__pitt
25 minutes 4 servings
$3.82
Ingredients
  • 1 lb sirloin steak tips
  • 1 tbs Montreal steak seasoning
Equipment needed
  • Cast-iron skillet
  • Chef's knife
Directions
  • Season steak generously
  • Heat your cast-iron pan on high
  • Cook steak 2-4 minutes per side for medium rare
  • Let rest before slicing
